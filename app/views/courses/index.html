Qualification options

You can apply for either of the following:

PGCE with QTS course

A PGCE with QTS allows you to teach in England. Unlike a QTS course, in Scotland, Wales and Northern Ireland. This qualification is also recognised internationally. Our PGCE course includes 60 credits that count towards a Master's qualification.

QTS course

You must have QTS in order to teach in a state school in England. {% endif %} {% if course.salary %}

Earn a salary while you train

You have the option to earn a salary with this course.

With a salaried course you:

  • won’t pay any fees
  • could have to pay tax and National Insurance
  • won’t be eligible for student finance

You'll be employed directly by the school and will be spending four days a week there, mostly in the classroom. Most people who choose this type of course are employed by the school once they qualify.
