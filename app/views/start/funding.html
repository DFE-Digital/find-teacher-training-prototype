Show me courses that come with a:

Salary

Earn a salary while you train. If you choose this type of course you don’t pay any fees.

Salaries are taxed. The precise amount you get varies – it depends on the training provider and subject.

Financial support for other subjects

Other subjects offer other types of financial support, besides salaries.

With a bursary you could get up to £26,000 tax free. You’ll need a 2:2 degree or higher to be eligible.

With a scholarship you could get up to £28,000 tax free. However, these are only available to outstanding graduates – you’ll usually need a 2:1 or higher, and to provide evidence that you’re passionate about the subject. Scholarships also give you access to networking, training resources and other support.

If you do get a scholarship you can’t get a bursary.

Find out more about financial support on the Get into Teaching website.
