6 School Direct courses folded into one

6 schools each modelled as individual courses on UCAS folded into a single course. Candidates apply using a unique course code and unique campus code.

If a provider models their courses this way it’s harder for the candidate to apply to the correct school in the UCAS application form. They see a list of 6 courses that look like: "Chemistry (2T9D)". There is no indication which school this course is for until they’ve selected one and looked at the campus dropdown list.
