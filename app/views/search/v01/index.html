Before you start

To apply to a teacher training course in England, you need to

  • have a British degree or an international equivalent degree
  • If English is not your native language, you need to pass the IELTS exam with a minimum score of 6.5 or pass other English test

Most providers also expect you to

  • pass the professional skills test
  • have a school experience
