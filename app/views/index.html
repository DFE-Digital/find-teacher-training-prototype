Find postgraduate teacher training for business studies

Use this service to search and compare postgraduate teacher training courses in England.

Qualifications and experience

To get onto a training course you’ll need the following qualifications, or the international equivalent:

  • grade C or above GCSEs in maths and English, plus a science subject if you want to teach in a primary school
  • a British degree

In most cases you’ll also need school experience.

If English isn't your first language you'll need to pass the IELTS exam with a score of 6.5 or higher, or an equivalent qualification.
